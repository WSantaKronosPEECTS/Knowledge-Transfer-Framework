## Color Palette
- **Primary:** #4A90E2 (Knowledge Blue)
- **Secondary:** #7B68EE (Innovation Purple)
- **Accent:** #50E3C2 (Energy Teal)
- **Background:** #1A1A2E (Deep Space)
- **Text:** #FFFFFF (White), #E0E0E0 (Light Gray)

## Typography
- **Headings:** Sans-serif, Bold, 48-72pt
- **Body:** Sans-serif, Regular, 24-36pt
- **Captions:** Sans-serif, Light, 18-24pt

## Animation Notes
- All transitions should be smooth (ease-in-out)
- Maintain consistent animation speed (1-2 seconds per major animation)
- Use particle effects sparingly for emphasis
- Ensure text is readable for minimum 3 seconds
- Background music should complement but not overpower narration
