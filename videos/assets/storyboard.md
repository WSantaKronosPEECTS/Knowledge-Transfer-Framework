# Video Storyboard - Knowledge Transfer Framework

## Scene 1: Introduction (scene1_intro.png)
**Duration**: 0:00 - 0:05  
**Visual**: Title card with animated Knowledge Transfer Framework logo and network connections fading in.  
**Notes**: Establish the theme with clean, professional graphics. Use brand colors and typography.

## Scene 2: Elastic Time (scene2_elastic_time.png)
**Duration**: 0:05 - 0:10  
**Visual**: Clock and timeline elements stretching and compressing to show temporal aspects of knowledge transfer.  
**Notes**: Emphasize how learning happens across different time scales - from moments to years.

## Scene 3: Entanglement (scene3_entanglement.png)
**Duration**: 0:10 - 0:15  
**Visual**: Interconnected knowledge nodes lighting up in sequence, showing relationships and dependencies.  
**Notes**: Demonstrate how knowledge in one domain affects understanding in another.

## Scene 4: Dark Matter (scene4_dark_matter.png)
**Duration**: 0:15 - 0:20  
**Visual**: Visible knowledge elements surrounded by subtle indicators of implicit, tacit knowledge.  
**Notes**: Use transparency and shadow effects to represent hidden but influential knowledge.

## Scene 5: Consciousness (scene5_consciousness.png)
**Duration**: 0:20 - 0:25  
**Visual**: Abstract flow of information coalescing into structured understanding within a mind representation.  
**Notes**: Bridge between individual cognition and collective knowledge systems.

## Scene 6: Learning Levels (scene6_learning_levels.png)
**Duration**: 0:25 - 0:30  
**Visual**: Layered structure showing progression from basic to advanced understanding.  
**Notes**: Clear hierarchy with knowledge flowing upward through each level.

## Scene 7: Applications (scene7_applications.png)
**Duration**: 0:30 - 0:35  
**Visual**: Split-screen or grid showing various real-world applications across education, research, and industry.  
**Notes**: Make it concrete with recognizable icons and scenarios.

## Scene 9: Closing (scene9_closing.png)
**Duration**: 0:35 - 0:40  
**Visual**: Zoom out to full network view with call-to-action and repository information.  
**Notes**: End on an inspiring note inviting viewers to explore and contribute.

---

**Total Duration**: ~40 seconds  
**Style Guide**: Modern, clean, scientific yet accessible. Use consistent color palette throughout.  
**Transitions**: Smooth fades and camera movements. Avoid jarring cuts.
