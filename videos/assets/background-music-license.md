# Background Music License

## License Information

This document describes the licensing requirements for background music used in the Knowledge Transfer Framework educational video.

## Permitted Use

### License Type
Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) or equivalent

### Allowed Activities
- Use in educational videos
- Non-commercial distribution
- Remixing and adaptation
- Commercial use (with proper attribution)

## Requirements

### Attribution
When using background music, the following attribution must be included in the video description or credits:

```
Music: [Track Title]
Artist: [Artist Name]
License: [License Type]
Source: [Source URL]
```

## Recommended Sources

1. **YouTube Audio Library** - Free music for content creators
2. **Free Music Archive** - Curated collection of CC-licensed music
3. **Incompetech** - Royalty-free music by Kevin MacLeod
4. **Bensound** - Royalty-free music with attribution
5. **Purple Planet** - Free background music for videos

## Music Selection Guidelines

### Style Recommendations
- Instrumental, ambient, or minimal beats
- Non-distracting, medium to low energy
- Tempo: 80-120 BPM for optimal pacing
- Genre: Electronic, ambient, or cinematic

### Volume Levels
- Background music should be 15-20 dB lower than voice-over
- Fade in/out at scene transitions
- Duck under voice-over for clarity

## Compliance

All music used must:
1. Be properly licensed for the intended use
2. Include appropriate attribution
3. Not contain copyrighted samples without clearance
4. Comply with platform-specific requirements (YouTube, educational platforms)

## Verification

Before final production, verify:
- [ ] License permits educational use
- [ ] Attribution is correctly formatted
- [ ] License is still valid and active
- [ ] No additional fees or permissions required

## Contact

For questions about music licensing for this project, please refer to the main repository or contact the project maintainers.
