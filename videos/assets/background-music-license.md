## Implementation Notes

### Audio Mix Guidelines
1. **Volume Levels:**
   - Background music: -18dB to -24dB when narration is present
   - Music alone segments: -12dB to -15dB
   - Fade narration transitions: 1-2 seconds

2. **Frequency Management:**
   - High-pass filter at 150Hz to avoid conflict with voice
   - Gentle compression (2:1 ratio) for consistent volume
   - No frequencies boosted above 8kHz to keep clarity

3. **Scene-Specific Adjustments:**
   - Scene 1 (Intro): Full volume for first 5 seconds, then duck under narration
   - Scenes 2-7: Maintain supporting role at -20dB average
   - Scene 9 (Closing): Gradual increase to -15dB for emotional conclusion
